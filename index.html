What is dhwani?

Dhwani is a smart sound recognition platform that can listen for familiar sounds, and alert you when it finds them playing again. Have difficulty hearing? Or want to keep track of sounds around you while you listen to music while wearing headphones? Dhwani lets you hear everything.

If you’ve ever been in a situation where you come home late at night and the only person to let you in is an elderly person like a grandparent who suffers from auditory loss and they don’t hear the bell. You keep ringing but they don’t answer so you’re left locked out of the house for the entire night. With Dhwani, this won’t ever be an issue again.

How does it work?

Dhwani uses audio fingerprinting technology to create acoustic models for any sound you need to keep track of. Our hotword detection engine allows you to pair any sounds you need to increase your productivity and stay attentive.

The platform can notify you if you’re name is being called out, the doorbell is ringing, your landline phone is ringing, and even supports custom sounds that you can label whatever you want. With smartwatch integration, it can detect irregularities in your heartbeat and send an SOS to your emergency contacts with three seconds so that action can be taken. Never let your hearing-impairment to get in way of your daily life again.
